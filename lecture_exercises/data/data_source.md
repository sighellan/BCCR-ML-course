# Data sources and licenses

## Energy consumption data

Electricity consumption data are obtained from **Elhub AS**, Norway’s national data hub for electricity.

- **Source:** https://elhub.no/data-og-innsikt/datakatalog  
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)

---

## Weather observations (MET Norway)

Meteorological observations are provided by **The Norwegian Meteorological Institute (MET Norway)** via Seklima.

- **Source:** https://seklima.met.no/observations/  
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)  
- **Licensing information:** https://www.met.no/en/free-meteorological-data/Licensing-and-crediting

The following station data are used:
- Folgefonna Skisenter  
- Bergen Florida station
- Weather data used in the streamflow project

---

## Mean sea level reanalysis data (ERA5)

Mean sea level data for the North Atlantic are taken from the **ERA5 reanalysis product**.

This material uses ERA5 reanalysis data from the **Copernicus Climate Change Service (C3S)**, produced by the **European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts (ECMWF)**.

- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)

---
